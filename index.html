About

A modern tailor with personal care.

New Star Tailors is based in Gongabu, Kathmandu. We focus on clean fitting, neat finishing and clear communication, so you always know what you are getting and when it will be ready.

  • Friendly one-to-one measurement and fitting for every customer.

  • Clear pricing before we start, with no hidden charges.
